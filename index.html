About Us

EnerGrow is an asset financing company based in Uganda that provides financial services to grid-connected micro, small and medium enterprises (MSMEs), enabling them to fully benefit from access to productive energy.


How We Do It?

Develop sector specific financing products with training and assets that are offered as a single product with low interest rates

Research and build a deep understanding of MSME industries

Use data to better identify potential customers and reduce our credit risk

Finance customers and track business performance

Stimulate higher use of productive energy through financing to drive local economic growth and accelerate grid extensions

Partner with key stakeholders
