About Us

EnerGrow is an asset financing company based in Uganda that provides financial services to grid-connected micro, small and medium enterprises (MSMEs), enabling them to fully benefit from access to productive energy.

Our Strategy

Access to energy for the majority of people living in sub-Saharan Africa remains an issue, but for those with a connection, only a handful truly benefit. Generally, grid-connected customers can afford electricity, but cannot afford the assets and appliances with which to make the most from that electricity. Prohibitively high costs of productive assets and the lack of affordable financing is hindering the social and economic development that access to energy should bring.

  • Problem
  • Solution
  • Market

Problem

  • Existing financial services are not fit for the majority of small businesses
  • Only 7% of businesses accessed a loan in the last five years
  • More than 80% of rural Ugandans with no access to electricity
  • Businesses can afford power, but they can’t afford the assets with which to benefit from their connection

Solution

EnerGrow currently targets five MSME sectors: carpentry, metal working, tailoring, restaurants, and retail shops and builds a deep understanding of each sector. This knowledge is the base upon which EnerGrow creates impactful, appropriate and affordable loan products for its customers.

EnerGrow creates loan products that comprise productive energy assets (e.g. sewing machines, fridges etc.) and key trainings that teach financial literacy, business planning, machine operation, safety, and business specific growth strategies.

Market

EnerGrow’s solution stimulates demand for customers connected to either the national grid or minigrids which is why both Umeme and Equatorial Power partner with EnerGrow

EnerGrow’s current market in Uganda is the 1.3m grid connections, set to increase by a further 3m in five years as Uganda targets universal access to energy.
